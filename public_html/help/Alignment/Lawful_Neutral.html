Help Topic: Lawful Neutral

Lawful neutral people support society and its laws because they value a safe,
orderly community.  Not many become adventurers as a result, but the few who
do tend to seek out bandits and rebels so they can bring them to justice.  A
lawful neutral person isn't without a conscience, but they will often consider
the legality of their actions rather than the outcome.
