Help Topic: Neutral Evil

A neutral evil person is selfish and callous.  Unlike a lawful evil
individual, he doesn't care much for laws and rules and will just as likely
knife a bureaucrat as bribe him, but he lacks the rebellious streak that
typifies chaotic evil people and is never evil for evil's sake.  He's evil for
his own sake.  A neutral evil person is cold and calculating and thinks how
each decision can bring him profit or pleasure.
