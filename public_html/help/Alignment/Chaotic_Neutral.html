Help Topic: Chaotic Neutral

A chaotic neutral person is care-free and wants to enjoy life, whatever the
costs may be.  Laws and morays that hold them back are meant to be broken. 
They range from happy-go-lucky wanderers to amoral hedonists, but they are not
evil or insane.  They just follow whatever whim takes them and follow their
heart.  Chaotic neutral people are often unhappy in normal society and
adventure as a way to be free to do what they want.  A chaotic neutral
individual doesn't always plan things through, but their nature is such that
they will help friends whom they've grown to love.
