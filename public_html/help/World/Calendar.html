Help Topic: Calendar


The Calendar
------------

The calendar is divided into 12 months, each with its own number of days,
which are further tracked by days of the week.

The list of calander events throughout the year will be listed here as they
are added.

Special Days of the Calendar

Jarul 1 - First day of the year, often celebrated as a day of good hope
Febil 14 - Anor's Day, the day most holy to the god Anor
Markel 21 - Spring Equinox, holy to the goddess Cynnis
Jundra 21 - Summer Solstice, holy to the goddess Tarafas
Sevrein 21 - Autumn Equinox, holy to the goddess Cynnis
Demmed 21 - Winter Solstice, holy to the goddess Tarafas
