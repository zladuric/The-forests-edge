Help Topic: Mounted fighting

DESCRIPTION

By taking advantage of your greater height and leverage while mounted, this
skill improves your defensive and offensive combat abilities.

As your mounted fighting skill improves, you will find it easier to parry with
either hand, use a shield, guard from your opponents' attacks, or help your
mount dodge more effectively.  You will also hit your opponents more
frequently, and cause greater damage when you do.

None of these capabilities is overpowering itself, but the combination of all
of them makes a skilled mounted fighter very formidable.
