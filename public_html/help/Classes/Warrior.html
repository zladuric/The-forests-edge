Help Topic: Warrior

  Warriors are skilled in the art of war.  Usually having dismal mental
abilities, warriors cannot cast spells of any type, and hence believe
almost solely in brute force.  At a young age, they learn how to
kick, bash, parry, and use a shield more effectively. 
  From there, warriors gain the important skills of rescue,
guard, and multiple attacks up to five, as well as the skill of using
another weapon in their off-hand.

  Due to their superior combat abilities, warriors are one of the
underpinnings of ANY successful group.

   To see a listing of the abilities available to Warriors, see the help
file on Abilities.
