Help Topic: Instrument Mastery

SYNTAX

play <instrument>

DESCRIPTION

After years of experience as an accomplished musician, the highly trained bard
can finally become masters of various instruments.  This skill allows the bard
to play certain instruments with such grace and beauty that his or her
audience will become more passionate for life, more aware of things around
them, or even more enlightened by what they've just heard.
