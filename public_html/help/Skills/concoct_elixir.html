Help Topic: concoct elixir

SYNTAX

concoct <spell>

DESCRIPTION

    Energy Cost: Spell casting cost + 10

As they become more proficient in the healing arts, clerics can also learn how
to prepare elixirs to aid their sick and injured followers.  The cleric must
first master the curative spell before they can attempt to bottle the spell.
Even then, the process can be a delicate one.  It is said that some clerics
have had flasks blown up in their face due to general clumsiness or just bad
luck.

See also help quaff.
