Help Topic: Song of the Sentinel

It's well known that young bards aren't always talented bards.  More than
a few have found themselves the instigator of a bar fight or two.  It was
during one of these bar fights that an unknown performer found inspiration for
this song.  He found that it cascaded a peculiar sonic barrier around him for
a short period of time, allowing him the chance to safely escape unscathed. 
Since then the song has been tweaked and transformed into the more adaptable
form now practiced by minstrels and military musicians across the land.
