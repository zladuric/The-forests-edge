Help Topic: Song of the Ward

The Song of the Ward is a masterpiece known only by the greatest loreweavers
in the land.  The complexity apparently doesn't stem from the song, but in the
performers own harmony with music itself.  Regardless, this powerful song
allows the minstrel to step out of the illusion of distance itself, pick out a
harmonious psyche and transport himself to that person.  The obvious benefit
of this spell is that it allows the already powerful bard to come to the aid
of any who have need of him, and allows that bard to witness even more legends
in the making.  Always at the right place at the right time it is said, and
through the song of the ward it is true.
