Help Topic: Song of the Wanderer

Bards are renowned for their wanderlust but very few bards realize how this is
connected to a song they are taught early in life.  Each minstrel feels oddly
compelled to sing and hum this same song to themselves throughout the rest of
their lives.  The song itself is slightly different between each bard, but it
is said that this song is never completed until the day the bard has fulfilled
his purpose in the world.  Alas some bards never see the completion of this
song, dying before it is their time.  It is said the incomplete song lingers
for eternity over the area where the bard fell into his final slumber.
