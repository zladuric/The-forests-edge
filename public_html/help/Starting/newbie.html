Help Topic: newbie

Welcome to The Forest's Edge!

Here are some things you should keep in mind while adventuring in our world.

  * We strongly encourage a role playing environment.

  * Our robust online creation system allows us to create complex quests,
    versatile weapons, intelligent mobs, and interactive entryways.  Fixes,
    updates, and adding new content can be done at any time.

  * Our door is open to suggestions at any time via the ideas board.

As a new player, if you get stuck or need help, there is a communication
channel that is seen by avatars and immortals, as well as other new players.
Simply type newbie and then your question.  For example, "newbie How do I buy
items?"

Here are the help files we recommend for new players:

  Starting Out
  Gates
  Towns
  Religions
  Roleplaying

The Starting Out file has plenty of helpful information and will give you some
great tips for success in our dangerous world.  It is highly recommended that
you take a look at it.
