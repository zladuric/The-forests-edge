Help Topic: Pkill

The official TFE policy enforced both by code and by the immortals is a game
free of direct player killing.  To satisfy the obvious conflict that exists
between races, clans and religions two major forms of pkill exist on the mud.

Zone
Three zones in TFE are currently open to player killing; these are the Elven
Village.  the Ice Troll Town and the Gnomish Picnic.  In addition, the Arena
Zone allows player combat without loss of equipment or experience points at
death.  Anytime in which you are in a Pkill capable room, a capital letter
'P' appears at the head of your prompt.

All three of these zones are not easily discoverable for the new player, so
you should not worry.  Nonetheless, caution should be taken when following a
leader who you do not know.

Important note: some of these zones have restrictions on summoning and recall
spells to prevent abuse.  Be careful to test out these spells when exploring
these zones for the first time so you are aware that recall, summon, and other
transfer spells may or may not work reliably in these zones.

Clan
The other form of Pkill exists when two clans declare and agree to a war
against each other.  During this time members of both clans are open to Pkill
from opposing clan members (as well as their own clan members for such cases
as a double-cross).

Any additional questions concerning Pkill can be addressed to either an Avatar
or Immortal.  Remember if you think something *MAY* be illegal ask an immortal
first *BEFORE* taking action.
