The above is the chart of level progressions for immortals.  The two-tiered
architecture reflects concurrent advancement for both builders and
admistrators.  Builders may perform some administrative functions, and
administrators may build if they wish.  Below are the descriptions of each
immortal position.

Implementor - Level 99
He who implements.

      *The owner of the MUD and the authority on such technical issues like
       issues like modifications of the source code and any tasks relating
       to the physical server the game resides on.

      *The ultimate authority over everything on the MUD.

Demigod - Level 98
This is the head administrator.

      *Managing and hiring all immortals and avatars.

      *Overseeing all projects.

Angel - Level 97
This position administers overall game balance:

      *Overseeing all spells.
            Balancing old spells.
            Creating new spells.
            Designing and implementing spell trainer quests.

      *Overseeing all skills.
            Balancing old skills.
            Designing and implementing skill trainer quests.

      *Overseeing races and religions.
            Integrating religion into the world.
            Designing and implementing race-based quests and content.
            Designing and implementing religion-based quests and content.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
The following positions fall within the building hierarchy.  The requirements
for achieving each is clearly listed after the description of the position's
responsibilities.

Please note that all area completion requirements are incremental, not
cumulative.  i.e.  to rise from an apprentice to a builder, you must complete
one assisted area, however that area does not count against the quota for
moving up from builder to architect.
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Architect - Level 96
This is the head builder.

      *Ensuring the integrity of the world environment.
            Approve all new zone creation.
            Oversee reviewing of all zones.
            Ensure that all zones mesh with existing content.
            Make sure that all rooms, mobs, and objects are of the highest quality.

      *Overseeing all builders.
            Checking on productivity.
            Answering building questions.
            Ensuring that apprentices are paired with acceptable mentors.
            Quality assurance throughout the building process.

      Requirements:
            *This position is appointed by the Demigod.

Designer - Level 95
This is a senior builder.

      *Creating complex content to enrich the world environment.
            Create and revamp towns as necessary.
            Implement quests at all levels.
            Design dynamic encounters.

      *Ensure a consistent level of quality throughout the mud.
            Review areas.
            Clean up typos on a monthly basis.
            Mentor apprentices.

      *Serve as a soft-code resource.

      Requirements:
            *Show a masters grasp of the building system.
            *Create at least 3 complex areas as an Architect that showcase the above
             requirement.
            *Review at least 3 new areas for content, quality, and consistency.
            *Design at least 4 dynamic encounters in a 3 month period.
            *Create at least 3 complex quests.

Artisan - Level 94
This is an intermediate builder.

      *Creating zones at all level ranges.

      *Mentoring apprentices.

      *Designing expansion terrain as directed by the Designer.

      Requirements:
            *Show advanced proficiency with the building system.
            *Turn out at least 4 areas of high quality that prove the above requirement.
            *Mentor at least one new immortal from apprentice to builder.
            *Display a willingness to assist builders and apprentices.

Builder - Level 93:
This is the newest builder.

      *Creating zones at all level ranges.

      *Assisting apprentices.

      Requirements:
            *Have a recommendation from the immortal to whom you were assigned.
            *Show moderate proficiency with the building system.

Apprentice - Level 92:
This is a training level.

      *Learning the building system.

      *Assisting an established builder with an area.

      Requirements:
            *Submit a storyline for a zone and five excellently written room descriptions
             that accompany it.
            *Have a history of good conduct as a mortal.

Note: This level is a shot at being an immortal.  Promotion and retention
are contingent upon initiative and quality work.  Apprentices are chosen at
the discretion of the Demigod.

Legislator - Level 96
This is a senior administrator.

      *Investigating and reporting on exploitable bugs.

      *Investigating any cases of cheating on the MUD.

      *Administering discipline to players when necessary.

      *Reimbursing players when necessary.

      *Coordinating the creation of helpfiles.

      Requirements:
            *This level is appointed by the Demigod.
