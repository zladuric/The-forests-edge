Help Topic: Osse-rev

Osse, Lord of Water, is the elemental god of water.  He controls the rivers,
lakes, and seas of the world as well as being the leading force controlling
most weather.  He sometimes appears as a large figure of water striding on
land, loosing neither form nor cohesion.

Osse is one of the four elemental gods along with Essyn, Kantos, and Kryoth.
When the world was created, Osse made the water with which life may thrive. 
Sst'rasa, God of Scaled Ones, is the child of Osse and Neughlen, though
Sst'rasa prefers to live on land rather than in his father's waters.

Followers of Osse may be of any alignment but usually are folk who live in
tune with water, such as sailors or fishermen.  His holy symbol is a stylized
drop of water, point up, or a stylized cresting wave.
