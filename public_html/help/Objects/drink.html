Help Topic: drink

SYNTAX:
drink             : Drink from an obvious water source.
drink <container> : Drink from a container.

DESCRIPTION:
Everybody's got to drink sometimes, no matter how tough they are, and
there's also many people that like to relax with a nice cold mug of ale,
or goblet of wine, now and again.  Whenever your thirst strikes, you
should always take a moment or two so that you can replenish your fluids,
otherwise you'll find your health deteriorating and you'll begin to heal
much slower and less effectively.  Whenever you plan to go adventuring,
always bring a good supply of liquids with you, and beware of tainted
drinks, because you never know whether someone may have dumped a bit of
poison into the local fountain or brewery.

NOTE: Not drinking will eventually result in death by dehydration.
