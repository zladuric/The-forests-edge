Help Topic: get

SYNTAX:
get <object>                    : Pick up an object.
get <object> [from] <container> : Get an object from a bag or table.

DESCRIPTION:
If you need something from a bag or see something interesting lying
around, you might just want to get it so you can put it to use.  Always
keep in mind that some things could weigh you down, or be too heavy for
you to even lift, and remember to open your bags and backpacks before
trying to get something out of them.
