Help Topic: drop

SYNTAX:
drop <object> : Drop an object on the ground.

DESCRIPTION:
When you don't have any use for an item anymore, it's time to get rid of
it.  If it's something valuable, and you don't want anyone else to have
it, you should probably junk it, but for everyday things, dropping it
should suffice.  Anything left on the ground will usually disappear
before long, many times in the clutches of a small child or a wild
animal, and other times another adventurer may come by and pick up the
object because they've found a potential use for it.  Either way, it's no
longer your responsibility.
