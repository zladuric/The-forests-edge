Help Topic: remove

SYNTAX:
remove <object>             : Remove an object from your body.
remove <armor> [from] <pet> : Remove armor from your pet.

DESCRIPTION:
Whenever you want to change or discard your equipment, you'll need to
remove some of it.  Occaisionally, though, you might have a bit of
trouble taking something off.  Clever (or careless) spellcasters have
been known to curse various pieces of equipment, so that it is impossible
to remove without using a remove curse spell.  Floating items can also
be a nuisance at times, and because of all their bobbing and weaving
around your body, can be very difficult to catch.  You will also need to
be responsible for the maintenance of your pet's armor.
