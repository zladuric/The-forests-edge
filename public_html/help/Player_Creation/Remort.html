Help Topic: Remort

Remorting is available to players who have attained experience level 90. 
Since advancement beyond level 90 is not permitted, most players choose to
"remort", meaning re-create their character at level 1 with bonuses.  The
player is allowed to retain his name, race, sex, alignment, and/or class if
desired, or may choose entirely new ones.

All money and equipment (whether worn, held in inventory, or in the bank) will
be retained.  Worn and inventory equipment will be moved to the player's bank
account.  Items you've customed that are size-specific will be re-sized to fit
your new character's body size.

All the player's pets will be removed from the game upon remorting.  Be sure
to retrieve any valuables they are carrying for you before remorting.

Upon remorting, the following data will be cleared: religion, clan, recognize,
reputation, quests.

The current remort bonuses include:

1.  An additional 50 creation points, allowing higher strength, intelligence,
wisdom, dexterity, and constitution.  The racial maximum value of each of
these statistics is increased by one each time you remort.

2. A 5% bonus to all resistances: fire, acid, etc.

3. An additional 250 pounds of space in the bank vault.

Contact an immortal if you have reached level 90 and wish to remort.
