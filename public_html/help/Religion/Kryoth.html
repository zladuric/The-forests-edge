Help Topic: Kryoth

Kryoth, Lady of Fire, is goddess of Fire, autumn, and the deserts of the
realm.  In rare writings is a physical form mentioned of her, and it is
usually that of a wraith-like form of fire.  Kryoth most often shows her favor
by flames that burn but do not consume, changing or growing foliage to trees
in the shades of autumn, or a hot, dry desert wind.

Kryoth takes as her own the element of Fire, the season of autumn, and the
searing deserts of the realm.  The brilliant, blazing hues of the trees during
the fall are said to be her touch upon the land, consuming the summer in its
flames, before yielding to winter.  She is a mercurial goddess, ever-changing,
flickering, much like her chosen element.  Her wrath is said to be frightful,
but slow to gather.  She counts no deities as her enemies, being more detached
from such human emotions, preferring instead to just be.

Kryoth's symbol is a stylized flame, often in shades of amber or crimson.

The Lady of Fire accepts only those who are willing to follow the cleansing
flame of fire.  As such, many of her priests are also gifted in the art of
fire magic.

Allowed worshipper alignment:  any
