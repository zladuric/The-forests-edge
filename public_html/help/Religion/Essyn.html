Help Topic: Essyn

Essyn, Wind Maiden, is goddess of Air, summer, and the plains of the realm. 
There are no recorded instances of her appearing in corporeal form, though she
has been known to show her favor in any strength of wind.  A particular
favorite method of hers is to produce a wind that levitates an object without
disturbing it as a sign of her favor.  She also employs this tactic to help
guide her followers, on the rare occasions she does.  Othertimes, a blue-white
glow is the only sign of her presence.  Ancient texts tell of her passage
leaving a solitary white-blue feather.

Preferring to remain neutral in the aspects of the gods, Essyn is remote and
rarely has contact with them other than her fellow elemental gods.  Essyn
rarely offers comment on any question, so that this particular issue is not
addressed is perfectly within her character.

The Wind Maiden's symbol has two forms...  one is a swirl of wind in blue on a
white background, and the older symbol is the blue-white feather on a blue
background.

Essyn accepts followers from any race and alignment, as the Air we breathe is
necessary for all life.

Allowed worshipper alignment: any
