Help Topic: The Twins2

  The Temples of the Twins were sought out and destroyed by elves and vyans
alike, until all but one were gone from the face of the earth.  That lone
temple was hidden by the Child-Goddess Anor, and placed beyond the mists of
time in the hope that one day, her sisters will be reunited.  Though it is
rumored that there are those who have found the Temple of the Twins and power
locked within it, such tales remain unproven.  Surely, however, should this
occur, the power wielded by the person who could reunite the Twins would be
near god-like.
