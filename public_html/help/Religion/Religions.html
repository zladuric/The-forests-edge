Help Topic: Religions

Religion is one of the main social and political forces of this world.

There are two distinct factions of gods, and also a set of gods who choose to
belong to neither.  The Gods of Light strive for the cause of Good, under the
leadership of Lonil.  The Dark Gods follow evil Temohpab, seeking to bring his
darkness to the realm.  The so-called gods of Neutrality are comprised with
both those who seek to balance the forces of Dark and Light as well as those
who simply wish to remain outside the conflict.

Gods of Light

Lonil, The Radiant One, God of Good and Light, Lord of the Gods of Light
Taranth, Lady of Peace, Goddess of Peace and Healing.
Tarafas, the Dawnstar, Goddess of Elvenkind.
Freylia, Goddess of Festivals, Harvests and Halflings
Cynnis, the Huntress, Goddess of Nature and of Ents.
Slen, Lady Luck, Goddess of Luck
Duroth, Dwarflord and Forgemaster, God of Dwarves and Metalcraft

Gods of Neutrality

Meriada, Goddess of Neutrality and the Arcane
Algesa, Goddess of Law
Morr, the Dark Angel, God of Dying; Ebon Triumvirate
Sst'rasa, the Scaled One, Goddess of the Lizardfolk
Anor, Lady of the Moon, Goddess of Love, Beauty, and Music
Kerog, the Red Wind, God of Battle, Tactics and War
Kryoth, Lady of Fire, Goddess of Fire, Autumn, and Deserts
Essyn, Wind Maiden, Goddess of Air, Summer, and Plains
Kantos, the Eternal, God of Earth, Winter, and Mountains
Osse, Rainlord, God of Water, Spring, and Oceans

Dark Gods

Temohpab, the Black Unicorn, God of Evil and Darkness, Lord of the Dark Gods
Morkith, the Undying, God of Demons and Unlife; Ebon Triumvirate
Xiroce, Lord of the Dead, God of the Dead; Ebon Triumvirate
Iniara, the Dark Mistress, Goddess of Hate, Pain, Lust, and Revenge
Ulthi, the Destroyer, God of Chaos and Destruction
Ashallan, the Masked, Goddess of Thieves, Stealth, Secrets, and Deceit
Lodar, the Black Star, God of Mischief and Cruelty
