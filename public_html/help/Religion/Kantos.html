Help Topic: Kantos

Kantos is god of Earth, winter, and the mountains of the realm.  Recorded
history tells of Kantos appearing as an old human male, with stately silver
hair and an air of timelessness.  His eyes are always brown, and he prefers to
be clad in robes of of the same shade.  Another method he has used to reveal
his presence is a soft, earthen glow limning an object.

Kantos is called the Eternal for several reasons.  He is god of Earth, winter,
and mountains, all things with an air of permanence.  Also, he has always been
concerned with the constancy of the earth -- that it remain whole, though it
may change, and not be destroyed.  He remains neutral in the sides of Gods,
choosing instead to focus entirely upon the earth, and not the forces that war
above it.  Rumor has it that for some strange reason, Temohpab has offered
Kantos the inexplicable boon to not destroy the earth, though recorded
history, scribes, and seers have no idea why.

Kantos' symbol is a brown triangle, point up, inscribed in a circle.

Kantos is non-discriminatory in his admission of worshippers, save they revere
the Earth.

Allowed worshipper alignment:  any
