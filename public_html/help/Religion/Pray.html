Help Topic: Pray

SYNTAX

pray                        : calls on your god to assist you
pray <player, pet, or mob>  : ask your god to assist someone else

DESCRIPTION

If you are in dire need you can try praying for assistance.  The gods of the
game might intervene on your behalf if you have been leading a pious lifestyle
and have not been calling on them too often.  This command does not contact
player gods but instead the god of your religion.  Some sample needs are
light, food, water, and hit points which the gods may give you in response to
your prayers.  The command takes no argument and your god determines what kind
of aid you receive.

See also: religion, religions, sacrifice.
