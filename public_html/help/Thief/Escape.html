Help Topic: Escape

Consider the humble thief.  Frail of body, he often finds himself overmatched
when stepping from the shadows for a fight.  He has no magic with which to
defend himself.  His best choice lies frequently in flight.  A thief who has
practiced the wise--not cowardly--discipline of escape, may find it easier to
leave his enemies behind.  Also, his opponents may also be less likely to
follow his route of...  wisdom.  The wise thief will also conceal himself
automatically if he can.
