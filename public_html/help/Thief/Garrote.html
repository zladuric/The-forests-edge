Help Topic: Garrote

SYNTAX

garrote <target>

DESCRIPTION

Using stealth and the garrote skill, a thief can sneak up behind an opponent
and strangle them with a specially-made instrument, also called a garrote. 
The garrote is typically a length of wire, rope, or cord with handles on each
end.

The thief's victim will take a large amount of damage from a successful
garroting, and will continue to choke and gasp for some time.  Speaking or
casting spells will be very difficult.

However, the thief will also be unable to use other weapons while garroting
his victim.
