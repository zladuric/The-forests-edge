Help Topic: Demon slash

This skill is only taught to the highest order of monks, whose knowledge of
the unarmed skills has improve to the stage whereby their pair of hands can
turn hard and sharp similar to those of the demon's.  The skilled monk will
tighten their fingers into a hand-knife and slash out at their opponents. 
Using this skill the monk is able to deal out massive damage to any opponent
and this skill becomes instinctive to highly skilled monk and will be executed
automatically in battle.
