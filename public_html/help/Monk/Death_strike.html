Help Topic: Death strike

The final combat skill in the monk's arsenal of combat skills, this skill is
shrouded in the mist of mystery as it is said that the monk who acquired this
skill can deliver a blow that is so fatal that it can claim the life of
another.  Also known as the forbidden art in the teachings of the monks, only
those of the highest orders of enlightenment may practice this skill.  This
special attack allows the monk to use an ancient technique that allows the
monk to directly draw energy from the shadow world and channel that energy
into a devastating blow that can easily cause fatal damage.  This technique is
extremely dangerous and once learned, it will execute instinctively with the
monk's subconscious and reflexes.
