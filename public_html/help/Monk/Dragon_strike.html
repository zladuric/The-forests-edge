Help Topic: Dragon strike

Dragon strike is one of the many skills in a monk's arsenal of combat skills. 
With this ability, a skilled monk is able intimate the claws of a dragon with
their hands and strike out at their opponent with devastating speed and fury,
many a time causing massive damages to their opponent.  A monk who has learned
this skill will be able to use it instinctively, executing it automatically in
battles.
