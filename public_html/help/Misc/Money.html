Help Topic: Money

The money system here contains four types of coins:
Copper
Silver = 10 copper
Gold = 10 silver
Platinum = 10 gold

In order to give coins to another pc, type:
  give <number_of_coins>*<coin_type> <pc_name>
    eg, give 3*gold greyclaw

To drop coins, simply type:
  drop <number_of_coins>*<coin_type>
     eg, drop 2*copper

Note: using the abbreviations: cp, sp, gp, pp, will not work.
