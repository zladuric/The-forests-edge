Help Topic: Banking

SYNTAX

deposit <amount>
deposit <items>
balance
withdraw <amount>
withdraw <items>

DESCRIPTION

The banking system provides a convenient mechanism for storing and retireving
money and equipment.  You must be in a banking institution to use these
commands.

The bank performs all monetary transactions in units of copper pieces, and
returns to you the correct distribution between all coin types.

The bank does not provide interest to its account holders, nor does it charge
any fees for its services.

TFE features an unusual form of branch banking; money and equipment in your
account is available for withdrawal at any banking institution across the MUD.

See also: money.
