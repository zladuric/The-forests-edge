Help Topic: Languages

SYNTAX

langauage <language_name>
abil l

DESCRIPTION

Languages are an integral part of the culture of each race.  Every distinct
race has its own language, and there are ten total.  In order for a member of
one race to communicate with another, both the speaker and listener of the
chosen language must have a good working knowledge of the language.  A lack of
language ability on either the speaker's or the listener's part will make
communications difficult if not impossible.

language <language_name> - Chooses the language you will currently speak.

abil l - Lists how well you know (in percentages) the languages.
