Help Topic: Introduce

SYNTAX

introduce                     : list introduced and recognized players
introduce <char>              : introduce yourself to <char>
introduce <char1> to <char2>  : introduces char1 to char2

DESCRIPTION

This command allows you to introduce yourself to another player.  From then on
they will see you real name instead of your appearance.  This mud strongly
encourages roleplaying.

You do NOT have to introduce to a character if you do not want to.
This feature is to foster a roleplaying environment, and bugging someone
who will not introduce to you is out of line.

If you don't want people introducing themselves (or anyone else) to you, turn
on option No.Intro.  This will also prevent anyone from introducing you to
someone else, which they can only do if consented anyway.

See also: options, consent.
